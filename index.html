Our story

Born from survival. Built to become something bigger.

Shockratees was created by John and Marcy after Marcy survived a stroke that changed daily life overnight. While navigating recovery, caregiving, financial pressure, and starting over, we chose to build something meaningful instead of surrendering to the weight of it.

Marcy helped create the name. John built the storefront and systems. Together, we shaped seven designs around energy, balance, voice, vision, and higher purpose.

When you wear Shockratees, you are not supporting a faceless corporation. You are backing two people who turned one of the hardest chapters of their lives into something visible, useful, and alive.

Before you order

Clear answers. No runaround.

How are orders fulfilled?

Each shirt is made to order by our production partner after your secure payment is completed. You will receive order and shipping updates by email.

How long does checkout take?

The first checkout request may take up to 60 seconds while our secure server wakes up. Please click once and allow the page to load.

What is the return policy?

Damaged, defective, or incorrectly printed items are eligible for replacement or refund. Because each item is made to order, ordinary sizing mistakes and buyer’s remorse are handled case by case. Contact us and we will treat you fairly.

How do I choose a size?

Use your normal unisex T-shirt size. When between sizes, choose the larger size for a more relaxed fit.
