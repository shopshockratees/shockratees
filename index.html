Our story

Born from survival. Built to become something bigger.

Shockratees is a veteran-owned brand created by John and Marcy during one of the hardest seasons of our lives. Between caregiving, financial pressure, health battles, and the weight of starting over, we chose to build instead of break.

Every design carries that spirit. The chakra symbols represent energy, balance, voice, vision, and higher purpose. The words are there to remind people that what they carry inside can still become something visible, useful, and powerful.

When you buy a Shockratees shirt, you are not funding a faceless corporation. You are backing two people who built this from the ground up with grit, love, and no room left to quit.
